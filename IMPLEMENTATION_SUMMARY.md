## 🚀 The 7 Viral Engines

### 1. Deal Brag - Shareable Images
- One-click Canvas API image generation
- 1080x1920px (Instagram Story format)
- Shows savings: "I saved $X on this [Car]"
- Pre-written social captions
- +200 points per share

### 2. Price Drop Alerts - FOMO Engine
- Animated price drop banners
- "🔥 Hot Deal" with pulse animation
- Live viewer counter
- Scarcity messaging
- Recent activity feed

### 3. Gamification System
**Points:**
- Sign up: 100 pts
- Save search: 50 pts
- Share deal: 200 pts
- Refer friend: 500 pts
- Contribute price: 1000 pts

**Badges:**
- 🎯 Deal Hunter (5 deals) - Bronze
- 📊 Market Insider (10 prices) - Silver
- 🤝 Helper (3 referrals) - Silver
- 🏆 Expert (1000 pts) - Gold
- 🚀 Viral Legend (20 shares) - Platinum

**Rewards:**
- 2000 pts = 1 month Pro free
- 5000 pts = Lifetime Pro
- Top 10 = Featured on homepage

### 4. Social Proof Engine
- Live deal counter
- Active user counter
- Activity feed: "Sarah saved $3,200 on BMW"
- Trust badges

### 5. Referral Program
- Unique codes (AHXXXXXX)
- Referrer: 1 month Pro per signup
- Referee: 50% off first month
- Dashboard with stats
- One-click social sharing

### 6. Conversion Optimizations
- Exit intent modal (+50 points)
- Sticky price alert bar
- Car comparison tool
- Best deal highlighting

### 7. Content Marketing Framework
- Deal of the Day structure
- Weekly market reports ready
- Best time to buy predictions
- Depreciation forecasts
